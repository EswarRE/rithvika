About Us

A dedicated team of professionals with wide exposure, experience in various fields as land acquisition, design and development of theme based concepts have drawn a vision of developing a heritage theme based project “nandanavanam.”

At Nandanavanam, we endorse our commitment to develop a township that matters just not another investment option, but as true residential living spaces with vibrant ambience, space management, heritage outlook to the care and comfort amenities and facilities that makes it a landmark project at Shabad, Hyderabad Telangana

Nandanavanam provides a holistic living close to a dedicated Sri Krishna temple complex (ISKCON Initiative) with guidance of a spiritual society.

We have a strong desire and commitment for developing Nandanavanam with all the specifications detailed and delivered to the satisfaction of the esteemed buyers.
